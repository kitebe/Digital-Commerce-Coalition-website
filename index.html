About the Coalition

The Coalition aims to strengthen people and planet-positive outcomes in India's digital commerce ecosystem by improving customer trust, supporting equitable economic participation, and enabling environmentally responsible growth.

People

Consumer trust, equitable participation, transparent practices

Planet

Sustainable growth, responsible practices, circular economy

Focus Areas

Strengthening Consumer Trust

Promoting transparency, responsible practices, and approaches that enhance consumer confidence in digital commerce.

Supporting Environmentally Responsible Growth

Encouraging and adopting practices that contribute to sustainable growth of the entire e-commerce value chain.

Broadening Economic Participation

Supporting opportunities for entrepreneurs, small businesses, sellers and other stakeholders to benefit from digital commerce.

How the Coalition Works

The Coalition serves as a collaborative platform for organisations engaged in or enabling digital commerce to work together on shared priorities.

Shared Perspectives

Developing shared perspectives and best-practices across the digital commerce ecosystem.

Thought Leadership

Producing thought leadership that supports informed policy dialogue.

Knowledge Exchange

Facilitating knowledge exchange and sectoral capacity building.
